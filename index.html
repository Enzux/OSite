History

If you’ve been playing Minecraft for a long time, you may remember the world that was in the background of the Minecraft Title Screen or Minecraft Menu Screen that you saw every time you started the game. It was added to the game in Minecraft Beta 1.8 and removed in Minecraft 1.13. The Minecraft@Home project finally found the seed almost 9 years later!
